About the issue

Why This Topic Matters

Economic growth is often associated with increased energy consumption, transportation, and industrial production.

Increased greenhouse gas emissions from these activities may contribute to climate change.

However, some countries are making efforts to lower emissions through environmnetal regulations, energy efficiency, and renewable energy.

Project Focus

This research investigates whether economic growth always results in increased carbon emissions across countries.

By comparing GDP, Population, and Greenhouse gas emissions data, this website explores whether wealthier or faster grwoing countries always produce more carbon emissions across countries.
